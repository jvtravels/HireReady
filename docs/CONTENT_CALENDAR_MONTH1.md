# HireStepX — 30-Day Content Calendar

> Created: 2026-04-28
> Owner: Founder / Content Lead
> Linked: `docs/SOCIAL_MARKETING_PLAN.md` (strategy), `docs/MARKETING.md` (voice)

A turnkey 4-week content plan. Every post is specified — hook, body
brief, CTA — so a content creator or you can produce it without
guesswork.

## Weekly Structure

**LinkedIn** (7 posts/week)
- 4 Static posts (Mon, Wed, Fri, Sun)
- 2 Carousels (Tue, Sat)
- 1 Video, 30 sec (Thu)

**Instagram** (7 posts/week)
- 4 Static posts (Mon, Wed, Fri, Sun)
- 2 Carousels (Tue, Sat)
- 1 Reel, 30 sec (Thu)

Total monthly output: **56 posts** (28 LinkedIn + 28 Instagram).

Best post times:
- LinkedIn: 8-9 AM IST or 6-7 PM IST
- Instagram: 7-9 PM IST or 9-11 PM IST

## Weekly Production Workflow

| Day | Task | Time |
|---|---|---|
| Sun (prev week) | Plan + write all 14 captions for the week | 2 hrs |
| Sun (prev week) | Design all 4 carousels + 8 statics in Canva | 3 hrs |
| Sun (prev week) | Record + edit 2 videos/reels | 2 hrs |
| Mon-Sun | Schedule via Buffer/Later | 30 min |
| Mon-Sun | Engage in first hour after each post | 30 min/day |

**Total time per week: ~10 hours.** If higher than that, hire a part-time editor (₹15-30K/mo).

## Repurposing Map

After publishing, repurpose top performers:

- LinkedIn carousel that goes viral → Convert to 3-Reel series next month
- Instagram Reel that hits 50K reach → Convert to LinkedIn video next month
- Top static post on either → Turn into a blog post (SEO compounding)
- Best testimonial → Pin to LinkedIn featured + IG highlights
