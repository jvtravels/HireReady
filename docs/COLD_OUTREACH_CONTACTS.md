## 📝 Workflow: How to Actually Use This

### Step 1: Pick batch of 20 contacts/day
From the verified table above. Don't try to email 200 in a day —
spam filters will burn you.

### Step 2: Verify 2 things before sending
1. Is the email still valid? Use [NeverBounce](https://neverbounce.com) (pay-per-verify, ~₹2 each)
2. Is the person still in role? Quick LinkedIn check — 30 seconds

### Step 3: Personalize the opener
The template in `COLD_OUTREACH_TARGETS.md` works only if line 1 is
specific to them. Examples:
- For VIT: "Saw your December placement report — 95% placement is wild"
- For Striver: "Loved your latest video on graph algorithms — the way you broke down DFS clicked for me"
- For Inc42: "Read your edtech funding analysis from last week, especially the part about D2C"

### Step 4: Send via Gmail (first 50)
Don't use bulk tools yet. Manual Gmail = highest deliverability.
Switch to Instantly.ai or Smartlead only after 50 emails when you
know what's converting.

### Step 5: Track
Add to a Google Sheet:
- Name | Email | Org | Sent date | Opened? | Replied? | Outcome

After 30 days you'll know which audience converts best — double down there.

## ⚖️ Compliance Note

India's DPDP (Digital Personal Data Protection) Act applies to
personal data processing. For B2B cold outreach:

- ✅ **Allowed:** Sending to publicly listed business emails for
  legitimate business purposes
- ✅ **Allowed:** Mentioning where you found them publicly
- ❌ **Not allowed:** Scraping personal emails at scale and sending
  unsolicited bulk mail
- ❌ **Not allowed:** Storing/processing personal data without a basis

Best practice: use only publicly published business contacts (like
the institutional emails above), include an unsubscribe option, and
never email more than 100 cold contacts per day from one inbox.
