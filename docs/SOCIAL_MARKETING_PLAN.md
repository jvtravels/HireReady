# HireStepX — LinkedIn & Instagram Marketing Plan

> Created: 2026-04-28 Owner: Founder Status: Draft Linked: `docs/MARKETING.md` (brand voice), `docs/LAUNCH_PLAN.md` (timing)

A focused, executable social plan for the two channels that matter most for HireStepX in India:

- **LinkedIn** = working professionals, mid-career, B2B (placement cells)
- **Instagram** = freshers, college students, early career

Voice rules from `docs/MARKETING.md` apply: confident, specific, no fluff, lead with outcomes, never use "revolutionary"/"disrupt"/etc.

### 3.4 Growth Tactics — LinkedIn

- **Comment-first strategy:** Spend 30 min/day commenting on 10 high-engagement posts in your niche (career advice, recruiters, product builders). Adds 200-500 followers/month organically.
- **Tag strategically:** Tag 1-2 people who'd genuinely benefit per post, never spammy
- **Engagement pods:** Join 2-3 founder/builder DM groups (5-10 members each) where everyone comments on each other's posts in first 30 min
- **LinkedIn newsletter:** Launch "Interview Intel" monthly newsletter after 1K followers. Each issue = 1 deep dive on an interview topic
- **DM after every signup:** Founder personally DMs first 100 paying users with a thank-you + ask for feedback. Highest conversion of any channel
- **Reply to comments within 1 hour** on first day (LinkedIn algo rewards this hard)
- **Repurpose top posts every 90 days** with fresh data

## 5. Cross-Platform Repurposing Workflow

Don't create fresh content for each platform. Build once, adapt 4 ways:

```
Founder writes ONE 300-word LinkedIn post (Mon)
   ↓
Designer turns it into 8-slide IG carousel (Tue)
   ↓
Editor extracts best line → 20s Reel (Wed)
   ↓
Editor cuts 5-second snippet → IG Story (Thu)
   ↓
Founder turns it into a Twitter thread + cross-post (Fri)
```

This gives you 5 pieces of content from 1 idea. Sustainable for a 1-2 person team.

## 10. What NOT to Do

Common mistakes to avoid:

❌ **Don't post the same content on both platforms unchanged.** LinkedIn text doesn't work as Instagram caption, and vice versa.

❌ **Don't chase virality.** Chase saves (Instagram) and meaningful comments (LinkedIn). Vanity reach without saves/comments doesn't convert.

❌ **Don't sell in every post.** 80% value, 20% promotion. Hard CTAs in 1 of every 5 posts max.

❌ **Don't use stock photos of "happy professionals shaking hands"**.Real product screenshots, real founder face, real user reactions.

❌ **Don't ignore comments for hours.** First-hour engagement determines algorithm reach. Block 30 min after each post for engagement.

❌ **Don't post and ghost.** Engage with 10 other accounts per post you make. Social media is reciprocal.

❌ **Don't use #interview #job #career as your only hashtags.** Too broad — your post drowns. Use mid-tail hashtags (10K-500K posts).

❌ **Don't pay for followers or engagement.** Instagram's algorithm penalizes fake engagement aggressively in 2026.

❌ **Don't run paid until organic baseline is set.** You need to know what works before amplifying.

❌ **Don't go quiet for 2 weeks.** Algorithm punishes inconsistency. Better to post 3x/week consistently than 10x/week then nothing.
