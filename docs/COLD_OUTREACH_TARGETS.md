```

with an AI that asks role-specific questions, scores their answers,

- Your students get unlimited mock interview practice (currently most

  ₹149/month — works out to ~3% of what students pay you

Happy to set up a free pilot for your next cohort first if that's
easier — no commitment.

Best,
[Your name]
[Phone]
[LinkedIn URL]

PS — Quick demo: hirestepx.com/demo (60 seconds)
```

## Why

Most cold B2C is spam. But targeted, value-first outreach to specific job seekers can work for early traction.

## Who to target (LinkedIn)

- Recent graduates from target colleges (LinkedIn alumni search)
- People with "open to work" badge
- Recent job changers (good word-of-mouth source for next switch)
- People posting about interview rejections / job search struggles
- Recent placements at target companies (potential affiliates / case studies)

## Template — Direct LinkedIn DM (B2C)

**Subject (LinkedIn DM):** Quick question

```
Hi [First Name],

Saw your post about [specific post or "open to work" status]. Job
hunting is brutal — wishing you the best.

I built HireStepX — AI mock interviews for ₹10/session, built
specifically for Indian job seekers. We have 3 free sessions, no card
needed.

Genuinely don't want to be spammy — if it's not relevant, ignore. If
useful, link is hirestepx.com.

Either way, good luck with [specific role/company they mentioned].

Best,
[Your name]
```

## ⚠️ Rules for B2C cold

- Maximum 30 DMs/day (LinkedIn flags higher volume)
- Never sell hard — give first
- No follow-up if they don't reply
- Personalize EVERY message (mention something specific)
- Skip if their last activity was &gt;30 days ago

## Math example (60-day batch)

- Send 200 bootcamp emails → 8-15 replies → 3-5 demo calls → 1-2 pilot deals
- Send 500 college emails → 15-30 replies → 8-15 demo calls → 3-5 pilots
- Send 100 LinkedIn creator DMs → 15-25 replies → 5-10 creators in program → 5-10 signups/day from each = 25-100 signups/day from this channel alone

**Ballpark expected outcome from a 90-day cold outreach push:**

- 5-10 paid B2B contracts (bootcamps + colleges)
- 15-25 active creator partners
- ₹50K-₹2L MRR from B2B
- 200-500 inbound signups/month from creator referrals
